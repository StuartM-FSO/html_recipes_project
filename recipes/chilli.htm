Chilli

The least Mexican food you will find

The favourite of people who think they are cooking Mexican food but are actually doing some fake version that no Mexican would ever touch. Serve with chips for the least authentic meal on the planet.

Ingredients

  • Mince
  • Random vegetables
  • Red kidney beans
  • Rice
  • Tinned tomatoes
  • Herbs and spices

Method

  1. Cook the veg
  2. Cook the mince
  3. Mix them together
  4. Prepare the beans so that they don't kill you
  5. Throw them in
  6. Plop the tinned tomatoes in and mush it all together
  7. Chop the chillis and throw in
  8. Boil the rice or just make chips
  9. Put it on a plate and plop the strange red mush on top

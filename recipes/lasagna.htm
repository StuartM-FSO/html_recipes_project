Lasagna

A tasty Italian treat

Making a lasagna couldn't be easier. Just follow the recipe below and the list of ingredients you will need

Ingredients

  • A load of mince
  • Sheets of lasagna
  • Chopped random veg like onions
  • Herbs and things to stop it tasting horrible
  • Tomato sauce to make it sloppy
  • Butter, one lump
  • Flour, a blob
  • Milk, one splosh
  • Cheese, a load of it

Method

  1. Fry the veg
  2. Fry the mince
  3. Mix it all together
  4. Plop in the tomato sauce and let it bubble together
  5. Melt some butter
  6. Mix in the flour and cook for a few minutes
  7. Splosh in the milk and mix together to make bechamel
  8. Layer it all together: pasta, mince, bechamel, repeat until done
  9. Grate a load of cheese over it
  10. Shove it in the oven
  11. Take it out before it is burnt
  12. Leave to cool down for about a week so you can eat it without it burning all your mouth skin off

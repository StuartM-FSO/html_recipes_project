Ploughman's lunch

Cheese, bread and salad

Perfect for middle class city types wanting to cosplay being poor agricultural workers on a visit to a Suffolk gastropub.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Bread (no baguettes or sourdough)
  • Butter, salted
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes (not cherry or plum)
  • Cucumber
  • Selection of pickled things
  • Chutney
  • Pork pie (optional)
  • Cider or beer (not optional, no IPA or fruit ciders)

Method

  1. Tear a load of leaves of lettuce and throw on a plate
  2. Chop tomatoes and cucumber and arrange delicately on top
  3. Take a very large wedge of cheese and place in the vicinity of the salad
  4. Slice bread and apply butter in a layer approximately 1/2 and inch thick. There are no metric equivalents suitable for a ploughman#s lunch.
  5. Tip a load of pickles on to the plate along with a generous blob of chutney
  6. Eat accompanied by beer or cider until legless
